The Property

Where History Meets
Modern Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Alton's Christian Hill Historic District, the Häagen Haus is a beautifully restored Italianate Federal–style brick building with records dating to 1861. Once home to the Häagen family — who operated Alton's largest mercantile business — this stunning property has been transformed into a fully-furnished, turnkey 4-unit guest house that seamlessly blends Civil War–era charm with modern comfort.


With highly-rated Airbnb listings, private brick courtyards, gas fireplaces, and panoramic views of the Mississippi River, this is more than a property — it's a legacy.

Heritage

A Story Written in Brick

The Häagen family built this home during the Civil War era. By 1885, Häagen had established Alton's largest mercantile business, operating in partnership with his sons Louis J. and Paul F. Häagen. Today, their legacy lives on — offering guests the ambiance of yesteryear with every modern amenity.
